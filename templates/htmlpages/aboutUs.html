About Us

VaxSafe is a student-developed web platform empowering Bangladeshi families with comprehensive vaccination management tools, bridging the gap to essential healthcare.
Our complete ecosystem ensures no critical dose is missed through intelligent scheduling, real-time government updates, and secure tracking for family members of all ages.
Deeply integrated with the national immunization calendar, we provide location-specific access to health centers and mobile units.
VaxSafe combines modern technology with local expertise to make family health protection as simple and reliable as checking your email.
