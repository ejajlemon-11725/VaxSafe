About Us

VaxSafe is a student-developed web platform dedicated to empowering Bangladeshi families with accessible vaccination management tools.
Our system simplifies healthcare by offering intelligent scheduling, real-time reminders, and secure tracking for family members of all ages.
Deeply integrated with Bangladesh's national immunization calendar, VaxSafe connects you to local health centers to ensure no critical dose is missed.
We make staying protected as easy as checking your email, contributing to a safer, healthier nation.
